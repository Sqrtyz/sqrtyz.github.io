Hello! I am Yuzhuo Tian, currently an undergraduate student from Zhejiang University majoring in Computer Science and Technology. At the same time, I am also pursuing a minor in ACEE program at Chu Kochen Honer College. My primary research focus on computer vision and spatial intelligence, especially on 3D Reconstruction, SLAM and Spatial Reasoning. I am also open to some other fields like robotics (VLA model) and world model. My long-term goal is to develop the full-stack intelligent agent with spatial intelligence, which is capable of perceiving the scene, reasoning about its content, and acting within it.

I am fortunate to conduct research in State Key Lab of CAD&CG with research professor Hao Chen and X-D Lab with assistant professor Yiyi Liao previously. Currently, I am doing an internship about 3D mapping at AI4CE Lab, New York University led by Prof. Chen Feng.
